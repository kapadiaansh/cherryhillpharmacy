OUR SERVICES

We can now prescribe for Minor Ailments. Minor Ailments are described as health conditions that can be managed with minimal treatment and/or self-care strategies. For more information, please feel free to visit OCP's minor ailment website.
Cherryhill pharmacy provides a comprehensive range of compounding services to meet your unique medication needs. Our expert compounding pharmacists are committed to delivering the highest quality compounded medications to ensure your health and well-being.
Having trouble organizing your medication? Let us do it for you! We can package your medication in an easy-to-use package which is so much easier than sorting through your Rx bottles. It also helps to keep track of your medications, refills, and any changes to your medication.

Enjoy the Special 20% Seniors Discount Every Tuesday on ALL Over-the-counter (OTC) products!

Our trained pharmacists are available to administer Flu, COVID-19, Travel vaccines, and other Prescribed vaccines in-store. Please book your appointment in advance to ensure quick and convenient service. Walk-ins are also welcome!
We offer medication reviews for all our patients as part of our patient-care services. These reviews aim to enhance your understanding of your medication regimen, identify and resolve any drug therapy issues, and improve your overall health outcomes.
Is it an inconvenience coming in for your refills, or are you too exhausted to make an extra stop after a long workday? No worries, we offer free Prescriptions Delivery anywhere in London, right to your door for your convenience, ensuring you never miss a dose!
